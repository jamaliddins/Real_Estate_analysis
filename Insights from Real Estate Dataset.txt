Key Insights
1. Rental vs Sale Price Distribution

No significant difference was found in the skewness of price distributions between rental and sale listings.
Both distributions exhibit similar right-skewed behavior (p < 0.05), indicating comparable pricing structures, though absolute price levels differ.

2. Price Variability Across Cities

Price distributions in major cities such as Riyadh and Jeddah show significantly higher variability compared to smaller cities.
Levene’s test confirms this difference (p < 0.05), with Interquartile Range (IQR) values in major cities being substantially higher, reflecting stronger market segmentation and diversity in property pricing.
